The engagement

Jeff tells the story:

We got engaged at Homewood Ski Resort in Lake Tahoe, CA. As usual, I winged it.

I got the ring the day before we left for our ski vacation, and even as the ski chair reached the top of the mountain, he had no idea what to do. Luckily, it was Marisa that prompted, "Hey, let's take pictures at this nice clearing area." Thank god for those Momma B photog genes.

After we snapped a few photos, with the incredible Lake Tahoe scenery in the background, I dropped to one knee. I started to stammer out the proposal I had been turning over in my mind, but before I could get even a few words out, Marisa completely lost it. Directions to Longlook Farm

Address: 195 Knox Mountain Rd, Sanborton, NH 03269

I93 North to exit 22, Sanbornton

Take a right off the exit onto Rte 127, go approx. 1.1 miles take a right onto Prescott Rd. If you get to the Sanbornton General store, you've gone too far.

Take your first left onto Weeks Rd. Follow for a couple of miles, you will pass DenBrae Golf course on your right. The road will split, stay to the right. Continue on Weeks Road until you reach Knox Mountain Rd on your left.

Take a left onto Knox Mountain Rd - this is a dirt road.

Follow Knox Mountain Rd. through the woods, you will come to an opening with a large brown colonial house on your left. Continue on Knox Mountain Rd , you will pass a yellow house on the right - take your next right - there is a green mailbox with 195 on it and lookfor the Longlook Farm sign. Turn into driveway. Go 1/4 mile on driveway, arrive at Longlook Farm.
